\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Service implementation for managing employee-\/related operations. This class provides methods for saving, retrieving, updating, and deleting employees, as well as filtering and restoring deactivated employees. It integrates with {\ttfamily \doxylink{interfacecom_1_1codingsy_1_1ems_1_1repository_1_1_employee_repository}{Employee\+Repository}}, {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1codingsy_1_1ems_1_1service_1_1impl_1_1_email_service}{Email\+Service}}, and {\ttfamily \doxylink{interfacecom_1_1codingsy_1_1ems_1_1service_1_1_audit_log_service}{Audit\+Log\+Service}} to perform database operations, send emails, and log actions for auditing purposes.
