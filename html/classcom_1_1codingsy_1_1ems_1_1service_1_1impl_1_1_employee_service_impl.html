Detailed Description

Service implementation for managing employee-related operations. This class provides methods for saving, retrieving, updating, and deleting employees, as well as filtering and restoring deactivated employees. It integrates with EmployeeRepository, EmailService, and AuditLogService to perform database operations, send emails, and log actions for auditing purposes.
